Gli strumenti di misura, quando proviamo a misurare un qubit, mostrano solo 0 o 1, con una certa probabilità.

No-cloning Theroem and Quantum Key distribution.

Entaglement: two entagled qubits always have the same measured value, **outcomes are correlated**. Also instantaneously a measure of the first qubit affects the second qubit, even at distance.

**Quantum Teleportation**: the quantum state of a qubit dissolves here and reappears there, on a different qubit.
By sharing a pair of entagles qubits, Alice makes really simple operations on a pair of one entagled qubit and a source qubit, then shares the (normal bit) result to Bob, which can then recreate the initial state of the source qubit, without translating qubits. This is the basis of quantum internet.
